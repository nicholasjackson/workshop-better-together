# Introduction

## 🚪 The Locked Door – Vault Secrets & Hidden Clues

## 🧭 Learning Objectives:
By completing this task, you will:

* ✅ Authenticate with HashiCorp Vault to retrieve secrets.
* ✅ Configure Vault that allows your Minecraft user access to a secret.
* ✅ Solve in-game puzzles to unlock the hidden door inside the tavern.
* ✅ Locate and open the treasure chest to retrieve the book of secrets for Task 3.

## 📖 Scenario:

You’ve arrived at Blackbeard’s Tavern, a mysterious hideout filled with secrets. Rumors speak of a locked door hiding a legendary treasure chest. To enter, you must solve a riddle to reveal the access policy stored in Vault. Once inside, another puzzle awaits, guiding you to a hidden book that holds the key to your next adventure.

Will you crack the secrets and claim the prize, or will you be lost in the depths of the tavern forever? 🏴‍☠️💀
